Dropdown arrow button In the BigQuery Studio pane, click the dropdown arrow button, hover over Notebook, and then select Upload. Upload notebook from GitHub To open the notebook, click your the dropdown arrow in the Explorer pane named qwiklabs-gcp-00-6fc852d3b127. Then click the dropdown for Notebooks.
